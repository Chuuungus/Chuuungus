Kaeya

"Needy little squeak"

Kaeya is what the internet would call "smol". She is the smallest in the family and the cutest meows in my opinion.

She will see you as her favourite grooming tool if she is comfortable being with you. Depending on her "needy metre" will either sit on your lap, chest or neck. Kaeya is aesthetically unique for her white chin. You can win her heart by being patient with her. Give the right amount of play and treats, and she will be your friend for life.
